# 📄 DocuMind User Guide

## 🚀 Welcome to DocuMind

**DocuMind** is an AI-powered Multi-Document Question & Answer system that allows users to upload PDF documents, ask questions in natural language, and receive accurate answers with source citations.

### Key Features

* 🤖 AI-Powered Question Answering
* 📚 Multi-PDF Support
* 🔍 Semantic Search
* 📌 Source Citations
* ⚡ Fast Document Retrieval

---

# 📤 Step 1: Upload Documents

### How to Upload

1. Open the **Load PDF Documents** section from the left sidebar.
2. Click the upload area.
3. Select one or more PDF files from your device.

### Supported Documents

* Academic Notes
* Research Papers
* Books
* Company Reports
* Technical Documentation
* Study Materials

Once uploaded, your files will appear in the document list.

---

# ⚙️ Step 2: Process Documents

After uploading PDFs:

1. Click the **Process Selected PDFs** button.
2. Wait for processing to complete.

### What Happens Behind the Scenes?

DocuMind automatically:

* Extracts text from PDFs
* Splits content into chunks
* Creates vector embeddings
* Stores information in a searchable knowledge base

This makes your documents ready for AI-powered search.

---

# 💬 Step 3: Ask Questions

Enter your question in the search box.

### Example Questions

* What is AJAX?
* Explain REST API.
* What are the advantages of Cloud Computing?
* Define Machine Learning.
* Summarize Chapter 5.

Click **Ask Question** to receive an answer.

---

# 🤖 Step 4: Review AI Answers

DocuMind generates concise and context-aware responses using information from your uploaded documents.

### Response Includes

* AI-generated answer
* Relevant document references
* Source page citations

### Example

**Question:**

What is AJAX?

**Answer:**

AJAX stands for Asynchronous JavaScript and XML. It allows web pages to exchange data with a server and update content without reloading the entire page.

**Sources:**

---

# 🔍 Step 5: View Citations & Context

DocuMind provides source citations for every answer.

### Benefits

* Verify information quickly
* Improve trust and transparency
* Locate information inside large PDFs

You can also inspect retrieved document chunks used by the AI while generating the answer.
